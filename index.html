Stat Kitty - your home for Roller Derby math.

Stat Kitty is an online resource for Roller Derby analysis. Five separate applications provide a toolset for tracking Roller Derby bouts.

Roster Builder:

Create and download a roster for your favorite team.

Jam Tracker:

Load your roster into Jam Tracker to track your bout in real time. Then, save your Jam Tracker file for analysis in the Jam Stats application.

Blocker Clock:

Load your roster into Blocker Clock to track your bout in real time. Then, save your Blocker Clock file for analysis in the Blocker Stats application.

Jam Stats:

Load your Jam Tracker file into the Jam Stats application and evaluate your bout performance.

Blocker Stats:

Load your Blocker Clock file into the Blocker Stats application and evaluate your blocker performance.
